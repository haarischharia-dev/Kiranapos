# KiranaPOS Business Playbook

## 1. The 60-Second Kirana Pitch

**Hindi Script:**
"नमस्ते सेठ जी! क्या आप आज भी पर्ची पर हिसाब लिखते हैं? ये नई मशीन देखिए। ये बिना वाई-फाई (WiFi) के आपके फोन से चलती है। ग्राहक का सारा उधारी (Khata) सीधा WhatsApp पर रिमाइंडर बनकर जाता है, और ये असली पक्का बिल (Professional Bluetooth Receipts) निकालती है। न कोई इंटरनेट की जरूरत, न कोई भारी कंप्यूटर का खर्चा।"

**English Script:**
"Namaste Sethji! Still writing bills by hand? Look at this new machine. It runs completely offline directly from your smartphone—no WiFi needed. All pending Khata (credit) balances send automatic reminders straight to your customer's WhatsApp, and it instantly prints professional thermal receipts via Bluetooth. No internet needed, no bulky computer required."

## 2. The ₹5,999 Economics Breakdown

- **Software Cost:** ₹0 (KiranaPOS runs free locally on the merchant's Android phone using SQLite).
- **58mm Thermal Printer Drop-Ship:** ₹1,500 - ₹2,000 (Wholesale from local electronics markets or online platforms).
- **Thermal Paper Rolls (Pack of 10):** ₹200.
- **Sales Margin / Setup Fee:** ₹3,799 (Your profit for configuring the app, pairing the Bluetooth, and teaching the merchant the workflow).
- **Total Pitch Price:** ₹5,999 (A massive discount compared to a standard ₹30,000 POS desktop system).

## 3. The Hardware SOP (Drop-Shipping)

When a merchant agrees and sends the UPI payment, execute these drop-ship fulfillment steps:

1. **Verify Payment:** Confirm the ₹5,999 UPI receipt hits your bank account.
2. **Order Printer:** Immediately open your third-party logistics portal (or e-commerce vendor like Amazon/IndiaMart) and order a standard 58mm ESC/POS Bluetooth Thermal Printer directly to the merchant's shop address.
3. **Share App Link:** Generate the production APK via EAS or send the Google Play Store link for KiranaPOS to the merchant's WhatsApp.
4. **Onboarding Support:** Once the printer arrives, call the merchant. Guide them to open KiranaPOS, select their language (Hindi/English), and click "Find My Printer" in the wizard. The app will pair instantly.
5. **Close the Sale:** Ask them to print their first test bill.
